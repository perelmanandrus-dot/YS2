How it works

One sale. Two people get paid.

Sellers hand off their stuff, hosts do the selling, and everybody walks away with cash. Here's the whole loop.

01

Seller lists an item

A seller with no time to run their own sale finds a nearby host, adds a photo and an expected price, and sends a request.

02

Host accepts it

The host reviews requests, accepts what fits their tables, and the item joins their sale for the weekend.

03

Item sells — both earn

On sale day the host keeps their agreed commission and passes the rest to the seller. Everybody wins.
